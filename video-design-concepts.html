Video Page Design Concepts

Concept 1: Responsive Video Grid

Description: Videos displayed in a responsive grid (2-3 columns). Each card contains an embedded video player with title, description, and tags below. Clean, Pinterest-style layout.

Best for: Showcasing many videos equally, browsing multiple videos at once.

Concept 2: Featured Video Player + List

Description: Large featured video player at the top with full description and tags. Below it, a list of other videos with thumbnails. Clicking a video in the list updates the featured player above.

Best for: Focusing attention on one video at a time, YouTube-style experience.

Concept 3: Two-Column Player + Sidebar

Description: Large video player on the left (sticky), compact video list sidebar on the right. Clicking items in sidebar updates the main player. Efficient use of space.

Best for: Desktop viewing, keeping player visible while browsing, professional/educational content.
